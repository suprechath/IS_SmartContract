General Questions

Resync is a platform that enables individuals and institutions to invest in real-world energy efficiency projects using blockchain technology. We connect building owners looking to upgrade their energy systems with investors seeking to fund these green initiatives and earn returns from the resulting energy savings.

Resync typically earns revenue through a combination of:

  • A platform fee on successfully funded projects.
  • A percentage of the project-specific RET tokens allocated to Resync for platform operations and development (as outlined in each project's tokenomics).
  • Potentially, a small operational fee deducted from the verified monetary savings before dividend distribution (this will be clearly stated in project details).

Our goal is to align our success with the success of the projects and our investors.

Resync aims to be accessible to a global audience. However, investors must comply with the laws and regulations of their respective jurisdictions regarding cryptocurrency investments and participation in such platforms. KYC (Know Your Customer) procedures may be required, especially for larger investment amounts, to adhere to AML (Anti-Money Laundering) regulations.

Investing

To invest in a project on Resync:

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Use a compatible Web3 wallet (like MetaMask) to connect to the Resync platform.
  2. Browse Projects: Explore available projects and review their details (overview, financials, expected returns).
  3. Complete KYC (if required): Follow the instructions for identity verification.
  4. Choose Investment Amount: Decide how much you want to invest in your chosen project (ensure you have sufficient stablecoins like USDC or USDT in your wallet).
  5. Confirm Transaction: Approve the investment transaction in your wallet. This will send your stablecoins to the project's smart contract and mint project-specific RET tokens to your wallet.

RET (Resync Energy Tokens or project-specific name like "Resync Alpha Building RET") are digital tokens representing your fractional ownership and right to receive dividends from a specific energy efficiency project funded on Resync. Each project has its own unique RET token. Holding these tokens means you are entitled to a proportional share of the profits generated from that project's energy savings.

Dividends are derived from the monetary savings achieved by the energy efficiency project. Here's the general flow:

  1. Energy savings are measured and verified by a third-party auditor.
  2. The building owner deposits the agreed-upon share of these monetary savings (in stablecoins) into the project's RewardDistribution smart contract.
  3. The smart contract automatically distributes these stablecoins as dividends to all RET token holders of that project, proportional to their token holdings. Dividends are sent directly to your connected wallet.

The minimum investment amount varies from project to project. This information is clearly displayed on each project's details page. We strive to make investments accessible with reasonably low minimums.

All investments carry risk. For Resync projects, risks can include: project performance being lower than expected (affecting dividends), smart contract vulnerabilities (though audited), stablecoin de-pegging, third-party (auditor/building owner) risks, and regulatory uncertainties. We encourage you to read the "Understanding the Risks" section on our "Learn / How It Works" page and do your own research before investing.

Blockchain & Technical

Blockchain technology offers several key advantages for Resync:

  • Transparency: All transactions (investments, dividend payouts, token ownership) are recorded on an immutable public ledger.
  • Security: Smart contracts automate processes, reducing counterparty risk and potential for fraud.
  • Efficiency: Streamlines the investment and dividend distribution process, making it faster and potentially cheaper.
  • Accessibility: Enables fractional ownership and participation from a global investor base.

A Web3 wallet (like MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.) is a digital wallet that allows you to interact with decentralized applications (dApps) like Resync on the blockchain. You need one to:

  • Securely store your cryptocurrencies (stablecoins for investing, RET tokens).
  • Connect to the Resync platform.
  • Authorize transactions (e.g., making an investment, claiming dividends).
  • Manage your digital assets. You control your private keys, meaning you have full ownership of your funds.

Resync plans to utilize efficient and scalable blockchain solutions, such as Layer 2 networks like Arbitrum or Polygon, to ensure low transaction fees (gas fees) and fast confirmation times for our users. The specific blockchain used for a project will be detailed on its project page.
